## Business Model (Critical Context)

ExitStudio is a long-term equity and revenue-share partner, not a temporary operator or consultant. When ExitStudio partners with a business, they embed operators, install systems, and remain active partners through every phase of growth. They do not "leave" after installing systems. They continue to resource the business, scale operations, and work toward an exit or continued growth on terms mutually agreed with the founder. Never write copy that implies ExitStudio departs after setup, has a short-term engagement, or sits as passive dead weight on the cap table. The partnership is ongoing, actively resourced, and built around shared upside.
